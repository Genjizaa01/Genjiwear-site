About Chef Genji

Chef Genji is a passionate and dedicated sushi artisan with extensive expertise in Japanese culinary arts. With a unique blend of traditional techniques and modern innovation, Chef Genji creates authentic Japanese dining experiences that captivate the palate.

Co-Founder & Recipe Developer:
Co-founder and head recipe developer for Ichiban Japanese Steakhouse - one of Houston's most prestigious Japanese dining establishments. Developed signature recipes and culinary standards that have defined the restaurant's excellence.

Co-Founder & Head Chef:
Co-founder and Head Chef of Segerto Lounge, bringing innovative Japanese cuisine and an elevated dining experience to Houston's competitive restaurant scene. Created signature dishes and complete menu development from inception to present day.
